Last night we had an impromptu BBQ at DSS, Nate’s shop. He was slinging burgers and we had just finished cooking and gone inside for a minute when Brian heard something outside and someone started banging on the front door. The BBQ grill was outside the back door in the little carport area.

To our surprise, we went outside to meet a fire truck and a few police cars. Someone driving by had seen the smoke from the grill coming out from under the carport area and thought the building was on fire. So without driving around, obviously, they just called 911. It was a little funny, though it isn’t good to waste their time. Here are some photos of the scene. Sorry they’re so dark…
