Well, my day started out really crappy, but it recovered by evening. I went to a chamber of commerce young professionals event at a local restaurant and met three lovely young ladies over dinner. They really lightened my mood and it was a lot of fun. Thanks girls! ![](http://shared.live.com/VIf!VWmJbs6tK-ObyYk28Q/emoticons/smile_teeth.gif)

After that I hung out with Nate for a while and then we went out with Brian to dinner again. I just picked at a salad and had iced tea. When we returned to the shop Nate proceeded to rip apart his server rack and rearrange and mount his servers. This had been on the old to-do list for a LONG time. I was bored so I took some pictures with my new camera. Here are some of them.
