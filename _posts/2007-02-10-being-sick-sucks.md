I almost never get sick, and when I do 99% of the time I work anyway. I’m that guy that wakes up and wanders over to the computer and plants himself there for the day when I don’t have to go out to clients’ offices. Today I went to a client for a little while to supervise a server RAID setup. *hacks up a lung* I’m just wondering how many other guys there are out there like me who do crazy stuff like that. Oh well, Sudafed here I come and sleep, hopefully. I hope I recover before Valentine’s Day. I’d hate to miss it, but I don’t know if I’ll see a valentine or just get the arrow in the rear. lol Anyway, for all those sick out there, keep kickin’. I sure will.
