Yesterday a few of us went on a hike up Cascade Pass. It was the most grueling, hailing, barefoot, miserable slog through sharp rocks, with 50lbs packs, I’ve ever experienced… Just kidding. It was actually a lot of fun and I took a lot of photos along the way, mostly on the way back down since I’m out of shape and was huffing and puffing the whole way up. I’m going to be sore from this hike for the next couple days!  
!(http://spaces.live.com/rte/emoticons/smile_regular.gif)

Anyway, the photos from our little adventure will be on my (http://www.flickr.com/photos/wforney/ "Bill's Flickr feed") shortly. As usual, I didn’t really edit the photos beyond rotating them and doing a few small tweaks here and there. Enjoy!

We went to Cascadian Farms’ roadside stand for ice cream and smoothies and a brewery on the way back for burgers and brats.

I’m going to go to our coffee thing in a few.
