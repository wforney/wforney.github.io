I went to dinner the other day with a friend of mine and we were joking around about how we eat out so much we should start a restaurant review. So, here’s the first one.

Espresso Connection is a drive-thru espresso and snack shop chain in the local area. We often visit the one on College Way in Mount Vernon and they are always happy to see us. Yesterday I took a picture of one of the ladies that work there and today I got another one.

I always get a surprise "whatever" drink when I go there, though I do ask for hot or cold most of the time. I have never been disappointed by a drink from there and I would highly recommend everyone try them out. I suggest a javarama if it’s hot or maybe a nice tea after dinner. They have an email club and a (http://www.espressoconnection.com/) too. Check ’em out, you won’t be disappointed! Oh, and the coffee’s good too… ;p
