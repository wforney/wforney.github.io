tonight I was watching a video and it made me think of taking up my book again. i haven’t written in a very long time and the world created by my imagination must be awfully dull without me there to tell its story. that is how i write you see, i "see" it and then i write it. the best stories are those that seem real enough to have happened. even the fantasy ones like LOTR. and so, i will continue my story though no one has bothered to read it. maybe some day someone will enjoy it. until then it’s just for me. i may post some of it if someone wants to read it.
