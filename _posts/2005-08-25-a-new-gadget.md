I got a new gadget last Friday!  A Samsung i730 and a 1gb SD card. It is a phone/PocketPC with bluetooth, WiFi, and all the little stuff you’d expect to find on something like this. The only thing it doesn’t have is a camera, but phone cameras usually have horrible quality anyway so I don’t consider it a huge loss. I got a bluetooth headset with it as well that has a 30 foot range so I can run around a bit. Steve got one too. This is a must-have gadget for anybody that is carrying around a phone and a palm/pda. I guess the geek factor just went up a bit more, eh?
